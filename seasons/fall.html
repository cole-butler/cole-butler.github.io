Autumn, the elk begin to bugle and the bow season opens. hunters flock to the woods to participate in the most extreme sport there is. other hunting seasons begin to open throughout the fall as sportsmen and women alike fill their freezers with game and have meat for their freezers. the weather begins to worsened and eventually gives way to the bitter winter.
